What is a primary source?
Primary Sources are immediate, first-hand accounts of a topic, from people who had a direct connection with it. Primary sources can include:

  • Texts of original documents
  • Newspaper reports by people who witnessed an event
  • Speeches, diaries, letters and interviews
  • Photographs, video, or audio that capture an event

*It is important to note that primary sources are the most valid of historical sources.
